# Sticky Footer

## Usage
### Use when
- Presenting one or two very important calls-to-action.
- The important call-to-action can't be shown directly within the view.
### Don't use when
- The call-to-action could instead be placed higher in the page structure, within the view.
- There are more than two important calls-to-action.

## Anatomy
- **Slots**: two predefined slots that hold the calls-to-action.

## Configurations
_Not documented as configurable properties in ZeroHeight._

## Placement
- Pinned to the bottom of the screen.
- When standalone it has a shadow; when combined with a [Bottom sheet](https://design.ns.nl/4a05a30ad/p/438def-bottom-sheet) the shadow is removed.

## Behavior
- The elevation diminishes as the user nears the end of the scrollable content.

## Best practices
- Keep to one or two items — don't crowd the footer.
- If the predefined setup doesn't fit, detaching is possible, but match the documented examples as closely as you can.
