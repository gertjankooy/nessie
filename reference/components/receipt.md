# Receipt

## Usage
### Use when
- The user needs information about the calculated price outcome while completing a form.
### Don't use when
_Not available in ZeroHeight — to review._

## Anatomy
- **Cost items**: the individual amounts.
- **Titles** (optional): group related cost items.
- **Total**: the calculated outcome.

## Configurations
### Sections
Sections can be removed without detaching the component.

### Cost subitems
The number of cost subitems in a section is adjustable.

## Placement
- Shown alongside the form whose outcome it calculates.

## Behavior
- The content always updates to reflect the choices made in the form.
- Use titles to group cost items only when there's more than one cost item.

## Best practices
- Give a clear indication when data in the receipt is updated.
